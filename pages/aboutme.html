Thanks for stopping by!

I’m a science graduate from the University of Toronto with a strong design background stemming from CyberARTS, a four-year secondary school program dedicated for budding creatives. By integrating my passion for science and design, I freelanced as an animator for two innovative med-tech companies, Hologic and Perimeter Medical Imaging AI. My work was integral in FDA clearance submissions, clinician training, user guides, and marketing. I established an excellent rapport with directors, and collaborated with clinical and engineering teams to ensure my work aligned with their vision.

Leveraging my professional experience and multidisciplinary approach, I branched into UX design. I’m confident in working alongside and learning from the brightest minds to cultivate digital solutions that bridge unmet needs. Most importantly, I enjoy the satisfaction that comes with seeing the lasting impact of a positive user experience. I look forward to working with you!
