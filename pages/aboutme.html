About me

Thanks for stopping by!
You're probably wondering how I got here.

Creative journey began here

Was part of CyberARTS, a secondary school program dedicated to budding creatives where I found my knack for design.

Explored my passion for science

Graduated with a BSc from University of Toronto in 2016, and honed my skills in gathering data, forming hypotheses, testing, and iterating - that sounds familiar!

Integrated my backgrounds

Freelanced as an animator for two innovative med-tech companies, Hologic and Perimeter Medical Imaging AI, producing support material integral to their products’ success.

Branched into UX

Leveraging 3 years of professional experience and a multidisciplinary approach, I branched into UX. I’m confident in working alongside the brightest minds to design digital solutions that bridge unmet needs. Most importantly, I enjoy seeing the lasting impact of a positive user experience.
