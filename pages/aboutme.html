About Thanvin

Thanks for dropping by! I recently led the end-to-end UX development of a web service at Abode Atlas, a B2M data-tech startup and second-round funding recipient of CMHC's Housing Supply Challenge. Prior to this project, I freelanced for three years as a Medical Media Designer for med-tech companies like Perimeter Medical Imaging and Hologic, where my visuals were key in advancing their surgical suite products to market.

Through UX, I enjoy the satisfaction that comes with seeing the lasting impact of a positive user experience. I also value working in an interdisciplinary environment, where I'm continuously learning from the expertise and skills of fellow team members.
